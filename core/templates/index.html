What we offer

Different doors into the same safe room.

One-to-one

Individual therapy

Weekly sessions for anxiety, low mood, burnout and everything harder to name — matched to the counsellor whose experience fits you best.

Together

Couples & family

Structured, judgment-free sessions to rebuild communication and trust, grounded in systemic and emotionally focused therapy.

For organisations

Corporate wellness

On-site and virtual workshops helping teams manage stress and burnout, with confidential one-to-one support for employees who need it.

For students

Academic workshops

Practical sessions for schools and universities on exam pressure, emotional resilience and asking for help, led by qualified counsellors.

How it works

Starting therapy, without the intimidation.

01

Tell us a little

Share what you're going through in a short, confidential enquiry — as much or as little as you're comfortable with.

02

We match you

We suggest the counsellor whose specialism best fits your situation. You're never assigned at random.

03

Meet at your pace

Your first session is a gentle introduction — no pressure, no long-term commitment. If it doesn't feel right, we'll help you find someone who does.
